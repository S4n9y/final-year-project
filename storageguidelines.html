Traditional & Modern Storage Techniques

  • Zero Energy Cool Chambers: Made from bricks and sand; keeps produce cool without electricity. Ideal for rural farms.
  • Metal Silos: Airtight, pest-proof, and easy to clean. Used for long-term grain storage.
  • Cold Rooms: Use for perishables. Recommended for cooperative groups or government-backed farm clusters.
  • Smart Sensors: IoT devices that monitor humidity and temperature can alert farmers via mobile apps to prevent spoilage.
