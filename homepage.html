Enhancing India's Agriculture

Empowering Farmers with Technology

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing innovative and tech-enabled solutions to enhance the primary sector of India – Agriculture. Our aim is to support farmers by improving logistics, crop processing, smart recommendations, and market linkages through digital tools.

Smart Crop Management

AI-based crop recommendation, real-time weather tracking, and yield prediction.

Produce Processing

Post-harvest handling, grading, storage management, and packaging optimization.

Logistics & Transport

Route optimization, vehicle tracking, delay/spoilage alerts, and buyer linkage.

Farmer Dashboard

Accessible insights, alerts, and decision-support tools in local languages.
