Information Extraction in Agriculture

Information extraction is the process of automatically retrieving valuable insights from agricultural data sources—like weather forecasts, soil reports, or market prices—using AI and machine learning models.

Crop conditions, yield predictions, pest alerts, irrigation needs, market trends, and government schemes can all be extracted from structured or unstructured data sources.

It enables smart decisions on sowing, harvesting, pricing, and resource allocation—maximizing profits and minimizing risks.

Data is extracted from satellite images, IoT sensors in farms, weather APIs, government reports, and agri-research articles.

Yes! Mobile apps and AI-based platforms are being built to deliver extracted insights directly to farmers—even in regional languages.
