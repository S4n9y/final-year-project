About Us

Empowering Smarter Agriculture through Innovation

Welcome to FarmHub

FarmHub is a smart agricultural platform created as part of our final year engineering project. Our goal is to help farmers make informed decisions by providing real-time data, crop recommendations, weather forecasts, and market prices â€” all in one convenient portal.

Who We Are

We are a group of final-year IT engineering students passionate about solving real-world problems with technology. Through FarmHub, we aim to bridge the gap between farmers and modern digital tools.

Our Mission

To simplify and support farming practices by offering:

  • AI-based crop and fertilizer suggestions
  • Early disease prediction using data
  • Live market prices and weather updates
  • Easy-to-use, accessible web design

Why FarmHub?

In a country where agriculture sustains millions, access to relevant information can make or break a season. FarmHub is designed to help even small-scale farmers adopt technology, maximize yield, and stay competitive.

Tools & Technologies

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend: Python (Flask or Django)
Database: MySQL / Firebase
Features: Weather APIs, Crop ML Models, Market API Integration
